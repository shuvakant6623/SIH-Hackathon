Integrated Platform for Crowdsourced Ocean Hazard Reporting and Social Media Analytics

Help protect India's coasts by reporting hazards and staying informed about ocean-related emergencies. Powered by INCOIS.

Report a Hazard View Dashboard

Key Features

Crowdsourced Reporting

Submit geotagged reports with photos or videos of observed ocean hazards directly from your device.

Social Media Analytics

Monitor public discussions on Twitter, Reddit, and YouTube to understand ground realities and information spread.

Real-time Dashboard

Visualize live reports and social media activity on an interactive map with dynamic hotspots.

Multilingual Support

Accessible in multiple regional languages to ensure widespread community participation.

About INCOIS

The Indian National Centre for Ocean Information Services (INCOIS) provides ocean information and advisory services to support disaster risk reduction and ensure maritime safety for coastal stakeholders. This platform aims to integrate citizen reports with INCOIS's early warning systems for a more comprehensive view of ocean hazards.

Contact Us

For technical support or inquiries, please contact the INCOIS team.

Email: info@incois.gov.in
